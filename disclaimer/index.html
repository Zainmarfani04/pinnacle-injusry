1. We Are Not a Law Firm

Pinnacle Injury Consultants is a legal consulting firm based in Houston, Texas. We are not a law firm, and none of our employees, consultants, or staff members are acting in the capacity of your attorney.

We help injury victims understand their options and connect with licensed personal injury attorneys who may be able to assist them. We do not practice law. We do not represent clients in legal proceedings. We do not provide legal advice, draft legal documents, or negotiate claims on your behalf.

Any attorney you work with through our referral network is an independent legal professional. That attorney — not Pinnacle Injury Consultants — is solely responsible for any legal advice, representation, or strategy they provide to you.

2. No Legal Advice

All content on this website — including articles, blog posts, FAQs, service descriptions, and any written or verbal communications from our staff — is provided for general informational and educational purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice.

Personal injury law is complex and fact-specific. What applies in one situation may not apply in another. Information on this site reflects general principles of Texas personal injury law as of the date the content was published, but laws change and your specific circumstances may alter the applicable rules significantly.

You should not rely on any information on this website as a substitute for individualized advice from a licensed Texas attorney. If you have a legal problem or question, consult a qualified attorney directly.

Contacting Pinnacle Injury Consultants by phone, email, SMS, or form submission does not create an attorney-client relationship and does not constitute legal advice.

3. No Guarantee of Results

We make no guarantees about the outcome of any injury claim or legal matter. Every case is unique, and results depend on a wide range of factors including:

  • The specific facts and circumstances of the incident
  • Available evidence and documentation
  • The applicable laws and how courts interpret them
  • The conduct and decisions of the opposing party and their insurer
  • The skills and strategy of the attorney representing you

Any reference to past case outcomes or settlement amounts on this website is provided for illustrative purposes only and does not guarantee, promise, or imply that similar results will be achieved in your case.

4. Attorney Referral Disclaimer

When we refer you to an attorney in our network, we are providing a connection to a licensed professional we believe may be able to help you. However:

  • We do not guarantee that any referred attorney will accept your case
  • We do not guarantee the quality, competence, or outcome of services provided by any referred attorney
  • The decision to hire any attorney rests solely with you
  • You should conduct your own due diligence before engaging any attorney, including verifying their license status with the State Bar of Texas

You can verify a Texas attorney’s license and standing at texasbar.com.

Pinnacle Injury Consultants may receive a referral fee or compensation in connection with attorney referrals. This does not affect the cost to you — personal injury attorneys we work with typically take cases on a contingency fee basis, meaning you pay no upfront fees and the attorney is only compensated if you receive a recovery.
