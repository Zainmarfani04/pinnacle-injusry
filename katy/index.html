Katy, TX Personal Injury Consulting — What We Do

Katy is one of the fastest-growing corridors in the Houston metro — and with growth comes increased traffic on I-10, the Grand Parkway, and FM 1463. Auto accidents, workplace injuries on commercial job sites, and slip and fall incidents in retail and warehouse facilities are among the most common injury cases we see from this area.

Pinnacle Injury Consultants acts as your guide from the moment you’re injured to the moment your case is in the hands of a qualified personal injury attorney. We handle the complexity. You focus on recovery.
